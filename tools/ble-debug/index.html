Voxa Cue Hardware Lab

Test the band before the app.

This page connects your Mac directly to the Nano. It proves whether the board is advertising, the BLE contract works, and the haptic driver accepts a command.

How the connection works

You do not pair Voxa Cue in macOS Bluetooth Settings. Chrome asks permission for one nearby device, then opens the BLE service itself.

1
Power and flash the NanoThe Voxa firmware must be running. Power alone is not enough.
2
The Nano advertisesFirmware broadcasts the name “Voxa Cue” and its private service UUID.
3
Chrome connectsClick Find Voxa Cue, select the device, and Chrome opens the GATT connection.
4
Send one commandThe Nano acknowledges it, drives the motor, then reports completion.
